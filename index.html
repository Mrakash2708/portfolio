Workflow

How we create
magic together

A streamlined, stress-free process designed to turn your raw footage into cinematic gold.

01

Footage & Vision

You share your raw footage and a brief outline of your goals, target audience, and preferred style.

02

The Rough Cut

I meticulously review all footage, removing dead air and carving out the core story and pacing.
