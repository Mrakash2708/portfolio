The Process

Simple, stress-free,
beautiful results

You've got enough to plan. Your editing process should be the easy part.

01

Share Your Footage

Send your raw footage via Google Drive or WeTransfer, along with a few notes — favourite moments, the vibe you want, any must-have songs.

02

Story Assembly

I watch everything carefully and build the emotional arc of your day — ceremony, vows, first look, speeches, dancing — into a compelling narrative.

03

Colour & Sound

Cinematic colour grading, music licensing or your chosen song, sound design and audio balancing to make every moment feel alive.

04

Your Film, Delivered

You receive a private link to review. We do up to 3 rounds of revisions until it feels exactly right — then your final film is yours forever.
